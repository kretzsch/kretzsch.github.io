About

I am Thomas Kretzschmar, a sound designer, composer, and audio developer based in Amsterdam. I specialize in crafting chill soundtracks and immersive sound effects for games and interactive media. My music has reached a global audience, with millions of streams on Spotify and a consistent monthly listenership of 30,000–50,000.

At Beyond Sports, I was responsible for introducing and building the entire audio experience from the ground up, implementing both Unity’s audio system and an FMOD audio package where previously there was no audio at all. Currently, I am developing a cozy game for Steam with a strong focus on music. My portfolio spans a wide range of genres, which you can explore further on my SoundCloud.
